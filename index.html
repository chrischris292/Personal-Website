Works
Baseball Sabermatrics Project
A Baseball Statistical Analysis Rankings Database
The project idea came to me after I saw the movie Moneyball. I had a fantasy baseball draft coming up with some friends and needed a way to rank players based off of statistical analysis that no one else could see. Sabermetrics analytics, created by statisticians and economists such as Bill James, often charge a premium to view on websites such as fan graphs. As a financially constrained college student, I had to find alternatives.
This project utilizes PHP, mySQL, HTML, Javascript, and Sean Lehman's Baseball database to implement statistical analysis in Major League Baseball.
Graham Valuation Database
A Financial Analysis Querying Interface
On the side of computer engineering, I have a great interest in finance. Since the 10th grade, I have managed investment portfolios such as Scottrade and Investopedia. As of August 28, 2013, my Investopedia portfolio stands at 84.69% positive. As a swimmer, I enjoy competiion and bettering myself. In order to improve my investopment portfolio, I needed a method to evaluate stocks in a different setting than already existed i.e. Google and Yahoo Finance; Benjamin Graham's stock valuation formula was a perfect way to do so.
This project utlizes YQL, HTML, Javascript, charts.js, jquery.js
