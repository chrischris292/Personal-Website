ABOUT ME
Christopher Chan
Computer Engineer at the University of Illinois
On May 18th, 2013, while visiting friends from back home, I sat in a Computer Science course at the University of Washington. This class was a web development course, CSE 154. The lecture was over Javascript and PHP. In a span of only 50 minutes I had discovered a new hobby. It was the first time I had ever become interested in programming. A few weeks and many course lectures later, I took two research internship positions with the Communications and Systems Biology departments at the University of Washington. Four months and many open/closed sourced projects later, I was hired as a Systems Engineering intern at the Dow Chemical Company.
